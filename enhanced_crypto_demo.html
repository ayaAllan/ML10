🎯 Project Overview

This project applies cutting-edge machine learning clustering techniques to analyze and group cryptocurrencies based on their historical price behavior, revealing hidden market structures and providing actionable investment insights.

🔍 Primary Objective

Discover hidden structure in the cryptocurrency market by grouping coins based on behavioral patterns using unsupervised machine learning algorithms.

💼 Business Value

Enable data-driven investment decisions, portfolio diversification, and comprehensive risk assessment tools for the volatile crypto market.

🔬 Research Impact

Study altcoin trends, market anomalies, and correlation patterns across different cryptocurrency segments for academic and industry research.

🎯 Goals & Objectives

Primary Goals

  • Market Structure Discovery: Reveal hidden behavioral patterns in cryptocurrency markets
  • Behavioral Segmentation: Create meaningful groups based on price action and volatility
  • Investment Framework: Provide actionable insights for different investor profiles

Specific Objectives

  • Identify high-volatility, stable performers, and Bitcoin-following cryptocurrencies
  • Create comprehensive risk-based investment recommendations
  • Develop sophisticated portfolio diversification strategies
  • Enable targeted market segmentation for investment professionals

Expected Outcomes

  • Reproducible methodology for ongoing research

🎯 Portfolio Diversification Strategies

1. Cluster-Based Diversification

  • Equal allocation across behavioral clusters (20% each)
  • Reduces concentration risk through behavior diversity
  • Balanced risk-return profile
  • Natural hedge against market movements

2. Risk-Tiered Portfolio

  • 50% Low Risk, 30% Medium Risk
  • 15% High Risk, 5% Very High Risk
  • Pyramid allocation approach
  • Suitable for most investor profiles

3. Core-Satellite Strategy

  • 70% in established coins (Bitcoin, Ethereum)
  • 30% in selected altcoins for growth
  • Balance stability with growth potential
  • Flexible rebalancing opportunities

4. Bitcoin-Correlation Based

  • High correlation (>0.7): Market followers
  • Medium correlation (0.3-0.7): Balanced exposure
  • Low correlation (<0.3): True diversification
  • Hedge against Bitcoin volatility
