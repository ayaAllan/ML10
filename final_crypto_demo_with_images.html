1. Cluster-Based Diversification

  • Equal allocation across behavioral clusters (20% each)
  • Reduces concentration risk through behavior diversity
  • Natural hedge against different market movements
  • Balanced risk-return profile across market conditions

2. Risk-Tiered Portfolio

  • 50% Low Risk, 30% Medium Risk
  • 15% High Risk, 5% Very High Risk
  • Pyramid allocation approach for controlled exposure
  • Suitable for most conservative-to-moderate investors

3. Core-Satellite Strategy

  • 70% in established coins (Bitcoin, Ethereum, top market caps)
  • 30% in selected altcoins for growth potential
  • Balance stability with growth opportunities
  • Flexible rebalancing and tactical allocation

4. Bitcoin-Correlation Based

  • High correlation (>0.7): Market followers for stability
  • Medium correlation (0.3-0.7): Balanced market exposure
  • Low correlation (<0.3): True diversification benefits
  • Effective hedge against Bitcoin-specific volatility
